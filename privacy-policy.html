Discgolfy – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Discgolfy (“we”, “us”, “our”, or “the App”) is a mobile application that helps users track their disc golf rounds, scores, and throws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely using industry-standard hashing via our authentication provider)

1.2 Gameplay and Match Data

When you use the App, we may collect and store:

  • Selected disc golf course
  • Friends you play with (other users you select in the App)
  • Scores for each hole and match results

1.3 Location Data (Optional)

If you choose to use the throw measurement feature, we may temporarily access your device’s GPS location to measure the distance of your throw. This location data is used only for this feature and is not used for continuous tracking or background location monitoring.

1.4 Technical and Usage Data

We may collect basic technical information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash or error information

This data is used to improve the stability and performance of the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Allow you to select courses and friends and record scores
  • Store and display your past matches and results
  • Provide the optional throw measurement feature using GPS
  • Maintain and improve the App’s functionality and reliability

We do not sell your personal data and do not use it for targeted advertising.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data to provide the core functionality of the App (performance of a contract) and to maintain and improve the service (legitimate interest).

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase as our backend service for authentication and data storage. Your data is stored on Supabase servers and protected using industry-standard security measures.

All communication between the App and our backend is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising. We may share data only with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the App (e.g., Supabase, Expo), solely for the purpose of providing and improving the service.
  • Authorities if required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the email address provided below.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.
